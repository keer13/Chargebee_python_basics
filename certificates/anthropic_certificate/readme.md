# Anthropic AI Fluency for Students

This folder contains my certificate and learning outcomes from the Anthropic AI Fluency for Students program.

## What I Learned

By completing this program, I learned:

- The meaning of AI Fluency and why it is important for academic and professional growth
- How to apply the 4D Framework:
  - Delegation
  - Description
  - Discernment
  - Diligence
- The difference between AI automation and AI augmentation
- How to create a personal learning context document for effective AI collaboration

## Understanding the 4D Framework

### Delegation
Learning how to decide which tasks should be handled by AI and which require human thinking and decision-making.

### Description
Learning how to give clear, specific, and detailed instructions to AI systems for better outputs.

### Discernment
Learning how to evaluate AI-generated responses critically by checking accuracy, usefulness, and reliability.

### Diligence
Learning how to use AI responsibly through verification, ethical usage, and careful review of outputs.
